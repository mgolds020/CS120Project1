The Band

For the past seven years, Project 5 has been putting the fun in funk! From Amy Winehouse to the Allman Brothers, this band has added a funky fresh feel to songs old and new. Throughout their musical journey, Project 5 has used their inspirations, backgrounds, and tastes to culminate a setlist for all ears, young and wise. Whether it's rock and roll classics, soulful ballads, or deep-cut indie tracks, Project 5 will keep your ears on their toes and get your feet groovin!
